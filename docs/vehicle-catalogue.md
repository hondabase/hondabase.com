**Notes & Conventions**

1. Generations are denoted by model years in the form `YYYY–YYYY` or `YYYY–present`.
2. Market-specific offerings are indicated in parentheses next to the relevant trims.
3. For motorcycles and automobiles the term "trims" is retained; the HondaJet uses "variants".
4. The original Chinese character trim name "游览" has been preserved for fidelity.
5. All model names are in lowercase for consistency with the routing structure.
6. Type R is listed as a trim within Civic, not as a separate model.
7. Brand specification is only used for the cars category where both Honda and Acura exist.
8. Generation information is ordered chronologically with the most recent at the bottom.

**Additional Context**

This catalogue represents the data structure that will populate the `vehicles.json` file, serving as the foundation for the knowledge base's hierarchical navigation system. Each vehicle entry will have associated articles (tutorials, how-to guides) that can be cross-referenced across multiple models when applicable.

For example:

- An article about K-series engines would be linked to multiple Honda models (Civic, Accord, CR-V) and Acura models (RSX, TSX)
- A general maintenance guide might apply to all vehicles within a brand or even across brands
- Market-specific content would be filtered based on the user's selected region
