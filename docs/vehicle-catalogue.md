## Database Structure Notes

1. **Generation Format**: Model years are expressed as `YYYY-YYYY` or `YYYY-present`
2. **Market Specifications**: Regional availability indicated after trim listings
3. **Trim Nomenclature**: Original manufacturer designations preserved (including "游览" for Gold Wing)
4. **Model Names**: Standardised to lowercase for routing consistency
5. **Special Editions**: Type R listed as trim variant within Civic lineage
6. **Brand Differentiation**: Applied only to automobile category where both Honda and Acura exist
7. **Chronological Order**: Generations listed from earliest to most recent
8. **Aircraft Variants**: HondaJet uses "variants" rather than "trims" designation
